# RCJ_KickSim Development Plan

## Goal

Develop a highly accurate physics simulator for RoboCup Junior Soccer Open solenoid kickers.

The simulator should predict ball velocity, spin, rolling distance, and determine the optimal kick height.

---

## Development Stages

### Stage 1
Development environment

### Stage 2
Striker dynamics

### Stage 3
Ball dynamics

### Stage 4
Contact model

### Stage 5
Rolling model

### Stage 6
Optimization

### Stage 7
Validation using experimental data

---

## Final Goal

A simulator that matches real robot performance.
